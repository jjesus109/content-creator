## Decisions Made
- scenes.json loaded once at SceneEngine.__init__ (not per pick_scene call) — consistent with CHARACTER_BIBLE module-level constant pattern, avoids file I/O on hot path
- Single GPT-4o call returns both scene_prompt and caption (not two separate calls) — saves ~$0.003/day, enforced via response_format={"type": "json_object"}
- mood returned from selected combo (not generated by GPT-4o) — ensures mood is always exactly "playful", "sleepy", or "curious"
- CHARACTER_BIBLE imported from kling.py rather than redefined — single source of truth for character consistency
